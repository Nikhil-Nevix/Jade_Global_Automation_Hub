# How to Download Professional PDF Analytics Reports

## Quick Start

1. **Navigate to Dashboard**
   - Open your browser and go to the Jade Global Automation Hub
   - Log in with your credentials
   - Click on "Dashboard" in the sidebar

2. **Configure Time Range** (Optional)
   - In the "Advanced Analytics & Insights" section, select your desired time range:
     - **Last 7 days** - For recent activity
     - **Last 30 days** - For monthly overview (default)
     - **Last 3 months** - For quarterly analysis
     - **Custom** - Pick specific start and end dates

3. **Download PDF Report**
   - Click the **"Download PDF"** button (üìÑ icon)
   - Your browser will download the report automatically
   - File name format: `jade_global_analytics_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.pdf`
